Monster Survivors

Dive into the action and see how long you can withstand the relentless monster horde in this thrilling survival game.

About the Game

Monster Survivors drops you into an intense, non-stop battle for survival. As waves of grotesque monsters close in from all sides, your only goal is to stay alive. You'll automatically attack the nearest foes, but your movement and strategy are key. Collect experience gems from fallen enemies to level up and choose from a variety of powerful upgrades to build a unique and devastating character with every run.

How to Play

  • Move: Use your mouse cursor to guide your character. Your character will follow the cursor's position.
  • Attack: Your character attacks automatically. Just focus on dodging and positioning!
  • Collect Gems: Run over the gems dropped by monsters to gain experience.
  • Level Up: When your experience bar is full, you'll be presented with new skills and upgrades. Choose wisely to enhance your power!

Key Features

  • Endless Hordes: Face an ever-increasing swarm of diverse monsters.
  • Roguelite Progression: Every run is different! Combine dozens of skills for unique builds.
  • Simple Controls, Deep Strategy: Easy to pick up, but challenging to master.
  • Fast-Paced Action: Instant, satisfying, and adrenaline-pumping gameplay sessions.

Play on Any Device

Whether you're on a PC with a large monitor or on your phone during a break, Monster Survivors is ready for you. The responsive design ensures a smooth and immersive gaming experience across all your devices.
